Books for Special Occasions

The best gift you can possibly give to someone special in your life

You have a teenager and you want to communicate those important messages before you let them fly out of the nest. How to do this in the most memorable, effective, and loving way? By writing a book especially for them. A birthday, graduation, or quinceanera gift, your unique book will incorporate your own messages, facts of life, stories from the past, your family's history, or anything that you deem important, in the form of a novel. It will be a real book, a magnificently told story that will remain in your family's future forever. Yes, it is an expensive and unique gift that only you can make but, with our Buk2Buk plan, your cost could be zero or even turned into a monetary gain.
